Installation

GNU/Linux

Extract the tarball and execute in the created directory: 
  $ make clean; make
Execution: 
  $ ./catch

Instructions

At start the game is paused. P both releases pause and pauses again. Arrows right and left move the glass accordingly, arrow down pauses the glass but not the rest of the game. Catching a drop awards 1 point, emptying the glass on the edges awards points according to second power of drops caught in the glass. Spilling over or missing a drop loses a life. Losing three lifes ends the game.
